REIT'2017: 1st International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies

Institute of Radioelectronics & Information Technologies оf Ural Federal University organized 1st International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies (REIT'2017). First session of the Workshop (2017) was devoted to the 65th anniversary of the Institute and covered two declared topics.

The main objective of REIT is to present the latest researches and results of scientists related to the field of Signals, Images & Texts Processing, Radio & Electronics and Distributed & Parallel Computing, to bring together researches and practitioners working in these fields and to share new ideas and results face to face.

We invite papers from researchers from all over the world. Remote participation by Skype is allowed, so if you cannot visit Yekaterinburg, you still can participate in the workshop.

The Workshop took place on March 15, 2017 at Institute of Radioelectronics & Information Technologies of Ural Federal University in Yekaterinburg, Russia. We have received 40 submissions; each of them has been reviewed by at least two Programme Committee members. The Programme Committee have decided to accept 12 papers.

Language

Official language of REIT'2017 is English. All papers should be in English. Working languages of workshop are English and Russian.
