REIT'2018: 4th International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies

Institute of Radioelectronics & Information Technologies оf Ural Federal University organize 4th International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies (REIT'2018 Autumn).

The main objective of REIT is to present the latest researches and results of scientists related to the field of Mathematical modeling & Information Technology, Digital Signal & Image Processing, Distributed & Parallel Computing, to bring together researches and practitioners working in these fields, and to share new ideas and results face to face. The advances in computer science and information technology were used to solve applied problems from areas of Mathematical Physics and Radioelectronics.

We invite papers from researchers from all over the world. Remote participation by Skype is allowed, so if you cannot visit Yekaterinburg, you still can participate in the workshop.

The Workshop will take place on October 24, 2018 at Institute of Radioelectronics & Information Technologies of Ural Federal University in Yekaterinburg, Russia.

Language

Official language of REIT'2018 is English. All papers should be in English. Working languages of workshop are English and Russian.

Paper submission

Authors are invited to submit papers in the PDF format written in LaTeX using the Springer LNCS style (LaTeX template download). The paper must be 7–10 pages including figures, tables, and references. We do not accept submissions in Microsoft Word. All papers should be in English.

Each paper will be reviewed by at least two Programme Committee members. We follow the post-proceedings publication model. Therefore, we will publish only those papers that are presented at the workshop. Final versions of accepted papers should include LaTeX sources.
