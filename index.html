REIT'2017: 1st International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies

Institute of the Radioelectronics & Information Technologies Ð¾f the Ural Federal University organizes 1st International Workshop Radio Electronics & Information Technologies (REIT'2017). This year Workshop is devoted to 65-th anniversary of the Institute and covers its two main research.

The main objective of REIT is to present the latest researches and results of scientists related to the field of Signals, Images & Texts Processing, Radio & Electronics and Distributed & Parallel Computing and to share new ideas and results face to face.
