REIT'2017: 2nd International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies

Institute of Radioelectronics & Information Technologies оf Ural Federal University organized 2nd International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies (REIT'2017).

The main objective of REIT is to present the latest researches and results of scientists related to the field of Signals, Images & Texts Processing, Radio & Electronics and Distributed & Parallel Computing, to bring together researches and practitioners working in these fields and to share new ideas and results face to face.

We invite papers from researchers from all over the world. Remote participation by Skype is allowed, so if you cannot visit Yekaterinburg, you still can participate in the workshop.

The Workshop will take place on November 15, 2017 at Institute of Radioelectronics & Information Technologies of Ural Federal University in Yekaterinburg, Russia.

Language

Official language of REIT'2017 is English. All papers should be in English. Working languages of workshop are English and Russian.

Paper submission

Authors are invited to submit papers in the PDF format written in LaTeX using the Springer LNCS style (LaTeX template download). The paper must be 7–10 pages including figures, tables, and references. We do not accept submissions in Microsoft Word. All papers should be in English.

Each paper will be reviewed by at least two Programme Committee members. We follow the post-proceedings publication model. Therefore, we will publish only those papers that are presented at the workshop. Final versions of accepted papers should include LaTeX sources.
