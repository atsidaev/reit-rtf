REIT'2017: 1st International Workshop on Radio Electronics & Information Technologies

Institute of the Radioelectronics & Information Technologies оf the Ural Federal University organizes 1st International Workshop Radio Electronics & Information Technologies (REIT'2017). First session of the Workshop (2017) is devoted to 65th anniversary of the Institute and covers two declared topics.

The main objective of REIT is to present the latest researches and results of scientists related to the field of Signals, Images & Texts Processing, Radio & Electronics and Distributed & Parallel Computing, to bring together researches and practitioners working in this scope and to share new ideas and results face to face.

We invite papers from researchers from all over world. Remote participation by Skype is allowed, so if you cannot visit Ekaterinburg, you still can participate in workshop.

The Workshop will take place on March 15, 2017 at the Institute of the Radioelectronics & Information Technologies of the Ural Federal University in Yekaterinburg, Russia. We have received 40 submissions; each of them has been reviewed by at least two Programme Committee members. The Programme Committee decided to accept 12 papers.

Language

Official language of REIT'2017 is English. All papers should be in English. Working languages of workshop are English and Russian.

Paper submission

Authors are invited to submit papers in the PDF format written in LaTeX using the Springer LNCS style (LaTeX template download). The paper must be 7–10 pages including figures, tables, and references. We do not accept submissions in Microsoft Word. All papers should be in English.

Each paper will be reviewed by at least two program committee members. We follow the post-proceedings publication model. Therefore, we will publish only those papers that are presented at the workshop. Final versions of accepted papers should include LaTeX sources.

Submit paper here: Paper submission is closed.
